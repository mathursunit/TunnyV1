📖 Tunny ke Qayde

Card Ranking (high→low): J, 9, A, 10, K, Q
Bidding: Clockwise from dealer; dealer bids last. Highest bidder picks trump from their hand.
Trump: That suit beats all others. If two trumps compete, the higher-ranked one wins.
Follow Suit: You MUST play the led suit if you have it. Playing another suit when you can follow = 4-ball penalty (colour cut).
Undercut: If trump is played and you have a higher trump, you MUST play it (unless your whole hand is trump).
Scoring: J=3, 9=2, A=1, 10=1, K=0.3, Q=0.2 pts. Counting team needs 10.5+ to win the round. Winner of last hand gets +1 bonus.
Balls: Win rounds to earn balls. First to 12 balls wins. Corner house = 11 balls (one away).
Jodie: Declare K+Q after winning hand 1 or 3. K+Q=2pts, K+Q+J=3pts (×2 if trump). Bluffing allowed — opponents can call Marials!
Tunny Call: Bet you win all 6 hands personally. 4 balls win / 4 loss / 8 if partner wins a hand.
Double: Called before hand 6 if you won all 5. 2 balls win / 4 loss. Banned on corner house.
Khanack: Requires a Jodie + opponent won ≥1 hand. 3 balls win (6 backward) / 4 loss. Raises target to 13.
Two to Clear: If both teams are 1 ball from winning (11-11), you need a 2-ball lead to win.
